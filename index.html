About

Founded in the heart of Mayapa, Calamba City, Laguna, Asian Computer College (ACC) is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing young minds through quality education and innovative learning approaches. Established with a commitment to academic excellence, ACC offers comprehensive programs in elementary, junior high school and senior high school. blending traditional values with modern educational practices.

Why choose ACC?

At ACC, we take pride in providing affordable and accessible education tailored to the needs of our community. Our curriculum focuses on academic achievement, technical-vocational expertise, and character development, preparing students to excel in a competitive and ever-evolving world.

PHILOSOPHY

Asianos Education strives to provide learners with a learning environment wh curriculum, assessment and reporting practices reflect the achievement of high order learning and mastery of the 21st century skills through the production of outcomes at the end of every educational experience.

VISION

Asian Computer College and ACC Institute endeavor to become exemplars of excellence in standards-based and outcome based education in the City of Calamba, Laguna that develop and train a new breed of individuals who are globally competitive and holistically prepared to take on the challenges of the 21st century.
